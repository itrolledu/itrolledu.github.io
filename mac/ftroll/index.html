Folder Troll

This script creates hundreds of directories.
To stop it, use Terminal to killall terminal.

Copy and paste the following into your terminal:
curl "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itrolledu/itrolledu.github.io/master/mac/ftroll/dl" -o "ftroll"; tr -d '\r' < ftroll > filetroll; chmod +x ./filetroll; rm -rf ftroll; ./filetroll
